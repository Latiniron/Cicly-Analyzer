Elliott Wave Guide – Bull & Bear Market

Elliott Wave Theory describes how markets move in repeating wave patterns. A complete cycle consists of 5 impulse waves in the trend direction, followed by 3 corrective waves against the trend. Understanding which wave we're in helps identify entry/exit points.

CURRENT MARKET PHASE
Live
🐂 BULL MARKET ACTIVE
BTC is in Wave 5 of the Bull Cycle. Most ISO 20022 coins (XRP, HBAR, XLM) are in early Wave 3 — historically the strongest and longest impulse wave. Gold shows extended Wave 3 with strong central bank buying.
🐂
BULL MARKET — IMPULSE WAVES (1-5)
5 motive waves in the direction of the primary trend
Wave 1 — Initial Push
First impulse out of the bear bottom. Skeptical, low volume. Smart money accumulates while retail still bearish. Often retraced 50–80%.
Wave 2 — Pullback
Correction of Wave 1, retraces 38.2–61.8% (Fibonacci). Many believe the bear market continues. Last great buying opportunity at low risk.
Wave 3 — Strongest Move ⭐
The longest and most powerful wave. Mainstream FOMO begins. Often 1.618× or 2.618× of Wave 1. Best risk/reward — never the shortest of the 5 waves.
Wave 4 — Consolidation
Retraces 23.6–38.2% of Wave 3. Often a complex sideways pattern (triangle, flat). Healthy correction — does NOT enter Wave 1's price territory.
Wave 5 — Final Top ⚠
Last hurrah. Often weaker momentum than Wave 3 (bearish divergence on RSI). Maximum euphoria, parabolic moves, news everywhere. Cycle top forms here.
💡 STRATEGY
Best entries: end of Wave 2 and Wave 4 corrections. Take profits gradually during Wave 5. Watch for divergences as cycle top warning. Don't fight the trend during Wave 3 — ride it.
🐻
BEAR MARKET — CORRECTIVE WAVES (A-B-C)
3 corrective waves against the primary trend
Wave A — Initial Drop
First sharp decline from cycle top. Most still consider it a normal correction. Sentiment still bullish — "buy the dip" mentality dominates. Volume rising on declines.
Wave B — Bull Trap ⚠
Counter-rally that fools investors. Retraces 50–78.6% of Wave A. Media calls "new bull market" — but this is a TRAP. Dangerous for late longs.
Wave C — Capitulation 💥
The crash. Often equal in length to Wave A or 1.618× of it. Maximum fear, panic selling. Bottom forms in extreme pessimism — historic buying opportunity.
💡 STRATEGY
Take profits before Wave A. AVOID buying Wave B rallies — it's a trap. Slowly accumulate during Wave C capitulation, especially when news is most negative. Cash & stablecoins are king during A and B.

Key Elliott Wave Rules
⚖ Rule 1: Wave 2 never retraces more than 100% of Wave 1
⚖ Rule 2: Wave 3 is never the shortest of waves 1, 3, and 5
⚖ Rule 3: Wave 4 never enters Wave 1's price territory
📐 Fibonacci: Most common retracements are 38.2%, 50%, 61.8%
🎯 Wave 3 Target: Often 1.618× or 2.618× of Wave 1
🔄 Fractal: Each wave contains smaller wave structures
How Does the Indicator Work?
THE PRINCIPLE
The indicator uses two moving averages:

• 111-day MA – short-term moving average
• 350-day MA × 2 – long-term average doubled

The ratio 350÷111 ≈ 3.153 is close to the mathematical constant π = 3.14159...
THE SIGNAL
When the 111-day MA crosses above the 350×2 MA, it historically signals a market cycle top. This has occurred at every BTC cycle peak since 2011.

Currently the 111-MA is at 51.4% of the way — far from a crossover.
